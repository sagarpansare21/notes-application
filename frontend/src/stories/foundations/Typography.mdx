# Typography

This page documents the typography guidelines and font styles utilized across the Notes Application. Our type system is designed to provide clear hierarchy, optimal readability, and a premium editorial feel inspired by Linear and Notion.

## Typeface
* **Sans-Serif (Default & Headings):** `Geist Variable`, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif.
* **Monospace (Code & Tech specs):** "Geist Mono", SFMono-Regular, Consolas, "Liberation Mono", Menlo, monospace.
